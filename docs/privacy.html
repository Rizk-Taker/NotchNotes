Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

No data collection

NotchNotes does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. Your notes are saved locally on your Mac and never leave your device.

No analytics

The app contains no analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind. There are no third-party SDKs, no crash reporting services, and no usage tracking.

No network access

NotchNotes does not connect to the internet. It requires no account, no sign-in, and no cloud sync. Everything runs and stays on your machine.

Local storage

Notes are saved to a folder you choose on your Mac. The app also stores your preferences (font size, notes folder path, file format) locally using macOS defaults. No data is stored remotely.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, open an issue on GitHub.
